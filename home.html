COURSES

North Negros College offers a diverse range of programs to cater to various interests. Whether you're passionate about technology, business, or hospitality, you'll find the perfect fit. Explore the exciting world of Computer Science, where you can develop innovative software solutions. Delve into the dynamic field of Business Administration and learn the art of strategic management. Or, embark on a rewarding career in Hospitality Management and master the skills to excel in the service industry. With North Negros College, you can shape your future and achieve your dreams.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) is an undergraduate degree program that equips students with the foundational knowledge and practical skills necessary to excel in the field of software development. This program focuses on the art and science of designing, developing, testing, and implementing computer programs.

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) is a versatile undergraduate degree that provides a solid foundation in core business principles. This program equips students with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in various business roles.

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM) is a degree program designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for a successful career in the hospitality industry.
