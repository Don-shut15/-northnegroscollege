BRIEF HISTORY

NORTH NEGROS COLLEGE, a co-educational and non-sectarian school, was established in 1979 as OWEN INSTITUTE OF ELECTRONICS by spouses ATTY. AGUSTIN SANTILLAN DESUYO and FABIOLA CHIONG DESUYO, offering Technical and Vocational courses. In 1983, it was duly incorporated and registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission as a corporation and known subsequently as OWEN INSTITUTE OF ELECTRONICS, INC. In 1985, by virtue of amendments of its Articles of Incorporation, Owen Institute of Electronics, Inc. was renamed as NORTH NEGROS COLLEG, INC. which offered Bachelor Degree Courses
